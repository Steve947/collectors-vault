Your collection is organised in three simple levels:

🗂  Collections — the top level, one for each thing you collect. e.g. "Diecast Models".
📂  Groups — sections within a collection. e.g. "1:18 Porsches".
📦  Items — each individual piece, with its photos, value, condition & notes.
